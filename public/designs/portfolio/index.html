About Apex Precision

Engineering Excellence Since 1998

Founded in 1998 by a team of veteran mechanical engineers, Apex Precision Manufacturing began as a small CNC machine shop in Detroit, Michigan. Driven by an unwavering commitment to precision and quality, we've grown into a 185,000 sq ft state-of-the-art manufacturing facility employing over 350 skilled professionals.

Today, we serve Fortune 500 companies and innovative startups across 42+ countries. Our investment in advanced technology, continuous training, and robust quality systems enables us to deliver components that meet the most demanding specifications.
