Our Story

Crafting Moments,
One Plate at a Time

Since 1998, Luna Restaurant has been a cornerstone of New York's fine dining scene. Born from a passion for extraordinary food and warm hospitality, we've spent over two decades perfecting our craft — blending classical French techniques with bold, modern flavours.

Every dish on our menu is a labour of love. Our farm-to-table philosophy ensures that only the freshest seasonal ingredients grace your plate, while our handpicked wine collection from the world's finest vineyards completes the experience.

Meet Chef Marco Bellini

A culinary luminary who trained under three Michelin-starred mentors across Paris, Tokyo, and New York, Chef Marco Bellini brings an unrivalled passion to every plate. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, honour the season, and let flavour speak for itself.

Having earned recognition from the James Beard Foundation and featured in Food & Wine's Best New Chefs, Marco continues to push the boundaries of fine dining with his signature fusion of classical French technique and bold New World flavours.

FAQ

Questions &
Answers

Everything you need to know about dining at Luna. Can't find your answer? Give us a call.

📞 Call Us
Do I need a reservation to dine at Luna?
+
Reservations are strongly recommended, especially on weekends. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability at the bar and lounge area. For groups of 6 or more, a reservation is required.
What is your dress code?
+
We encourage smart casual to formal attire. We kindly ask that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, flip-flops, or beachwear. Business casual is always welcome.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
+
Absolutely. We cater to vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and all major dietary requirements. Please inform us of any allergies or restrictions when making your reservation so our kitchen can prepare accordingly.
Do you offer private dining?
+
Yes. Our private dining room accommodates up to 20 guests and can be tailored for corporate events, celebrations, and intimate gatherings. A bespoke menu and dedicated service team are included.
Is there parking available?
+
We offer complimentary valet parking for all diners. Street parking is also available on Gourmet Street. Our valet attendants are available from 4:30 PM daily.
What is your cancellation policy?
+
We kindly request 24 hours notice for cancellations. For parties of 6 or more, 48 hours notice is required. Late cancellations may be subject to a cancellation fee of $25 per person.
